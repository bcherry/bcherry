This deck is from early 2010, and accompanied a small internal tech talk I gave at Slide (RIP) that year. I posted it online afterwards because some of the engineers had expressed interest in keeping it handy.

As such, this deck was never really intended for mass consumption, which is why it's sparse, and why it doesn't have handy things like navigation controls. I built it with Showoff, but it's a static copy of it that's being hosted by my web server.

Also, at that time, I'd only been really doing serious JavaScript for a few months, and was just sharing a lot of stuff I've discovered in the context of Slide's applications with other engineers, to help us all improve (we didn't really have any JS experts on the team).

So if anything looks weird, or misleading, or incomplete, it's either due to my inexperience (at the time) or some particularities of the Slide stack (at the time).

I'm sorry it's missing benchmarks, and some of the info is out of date because browsers change so quickly. But I do hope it's at least a little bit useful!
